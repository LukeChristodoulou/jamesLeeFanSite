About Me

James Lee Animation is an award winning production studio, creating animated content for clients such as Qantas, Amnesty International and the Australian Government. Started in 2007 by Director James Lee, after his film ‘Tarboy’ gained notoriety, his independent works have generated over ten million hits online. The studio has delivered animation in TV, Online Advertising, Video Game Cinematics and has screened at Siggraph Asia and the Game Awards in Los Angeles.
